Lisk Italian Group

LIG (Lisk Italian Group) is the working union of 8 Italian delegates who have begun to know each other and work together on several fronts, thanks to Lisk and his first testnet. Delegates concerned you know them all and they are: Anamix, Dakk, Liskit, Corsaro, Gregorst, RedSn0w, Ondin and Splatters, all Italians and all committed to manage and maintain their nodes up h24, of course interacting with the rest of the World Community Lisk. The intention of this group is funding for development projects and Dapp of LIG, to be reported in full on this site where also we present and monitor every step forward, every single project of the LIG and also all external developers who want to add the incoming platform.
